Prevention Tips

  • Wear a mask that covers your nose and mouth in public settings.
  • Stay at least 6 feet away from others, especially indoors.
  • Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Avoid touching your face, especially your eyes, nose, and mouth.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched surfaces daily.

COVID-19 Symptoms

Common symptoms of COVID-19 include:

  • Fever or chills
  • Cough
  • Shortness of breath or difficulty breathing
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle or body aches
  • Loss of taste or smell

If you experience severe symptoms, such as trouble breathing, seek emergency medical care immediately.

Vaccination Guidance

Vaccination is a key measure to prevent severe illness. The following guidelines apply:

  • Check with local health authorities for vaccination eligibility and locations.
  • Stay up-to-date with booster shots as recommended.
  • Continue practicing preventive measures, even if vaccinated, to protect others.
